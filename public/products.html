Welcome to Munamii Cakery

The place of unusual cupcakes and distinctive cakes

We’re passionate about cupcakes, and love designing, making and delivering our delicious gifts and treats to happy people all around the Sweden.
If you want to treat someone (or yourself!) to our cupcakes, simply choose from our extensive range of flavours and order securely online – your cupcakes will be delivered by courier the next day or on another date of your choice.

You can order our cupcakes in attractive presentation boxes of 6 or 12, and if you wish, we can include a personalised gift message.
